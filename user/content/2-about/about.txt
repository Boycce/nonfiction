#The Studio
Nonfiction is a San Francisco based design studio specializing in industrial design, branding, strategy and packaging design.

Founded in 2016 by Phnam and Mardis Bagley, we have been engaging experiences through the design of fun and sophisticated products.

Our clients range from large corporations, mid-size companies and funded startups from all over the world.
We also leverage our relationships with engineering and manufacturing partners to help execute your ideas on time and on budget.

Owner1: 

### Phnam Bagley

Phnam is a creative director who creates products in a very wide range of product categories.
She started her career designing transportation and habitats for manned space missions, then turned to consumer electronics while crafting leather goods by hand.

She has designed and led teams at varied design consultancies including IDEO, Lunar, Matter, Enlisted and Lifestyledesign. 
Phnam is the CEO and Design Director at Eternal Luxe, a brand of luxury goods made in USA.
She also teaches industrial design at California College of the Arts and mentors at Founder Institute.

Hometown: Paris, France
Education: Strate (France)
Designskolen Kolding (Denmark)
SICSA U. of Houston (USA)
Amblard Leather Atelier (USA)

----

Owner2: 

### Mardis Bagley

Mardis is an industrial design director with 10 years experience in consumer electronics, lifestyle, soft goods and packaging. 

Prior to his career in industrial design, Mardis worked for 15 years in the graphic design field, managing branding and digital design teams. Mardis specializes in fast, technical execution and shepherds products all the way through mass production, to insure design intent. He has worked on consumer products for Nike, Dell, Intel, Logitech, Symantec, Facebook and for mid-sized companies and startups too numerous to count.

Hometown: Champaign, Illinois, USA
Education: University of Houston (USA)
University of Illinois (USA)
